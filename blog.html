Rolls‑Royce Phantom

Overview

The Phantom is the benchmark for ultra‑luxury sedans. It focuses on serene travel, bespoke craftsmanship, and a near‑silent cabin. Almost every surface can be tailored to the owner, from unique paint to custom art in the dashboard gallery.

Why It’s Special

  • “Magic Carpet” ride feel with exceptional isolation from noise and vibration.
  • Coach doors with soft‑close and effortless entry/exit.
  • Endless personalization through the brand’s bespoke program.
